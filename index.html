About SafeReport

SafeReport is a privacy-first platform for reporting harassment incidents safely and anonymously. We prioritize victim safety and privacy above all else.

How We Protect Your Privacy

  • No identifying information is ever shown publicly
  • Optional contact information is encrypted
  • Only authorized personnel can access full case details
  • All data is stored securely with encryption

Our Process

  1. You submit a report with as much or as little detail as you're comfortable with
  2. We assign your case to the appropriate authority
  3. You receive a case ID to track progress (if you provided contact info)
  4. The assigned authority reviews and takes appropriate action
